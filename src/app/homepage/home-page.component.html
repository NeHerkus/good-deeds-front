We started the "Good Works Summer" project last year,
with over 1,400 colleagues supporting this project every year.
We can change environment and society a lot, so we invite you to participate!

Team

Who can participate?

All employees of Bank can participate in the project “Good Work Summer”.

How to participate?

Swing to teams. Participants are invited to do a good job together, sailing into teams. The captain has to register the team - no other members have to register separately. We will approve your participation in the project by email.

Job

What support is available?

Each employee receives an amount of EUR 15, which can be used to perform a good job (eg purchase the necessary tools, etc.).

How to choose a good job?

You can choose activities that seem meaningful and important to you. Let us carefully consider who needs our time, strength, advice, help, understanding. Let's go to the nearest communities, our native cities, let's talk to nearby organizations, neighborhoods, hard-working families. Maybe they need help?

Time

When to volunteer?

In 2019, you can register for the project and do a good job in May-October. Each employee has one free and paid working day for good work.

What's next?

After a good job, the team captain is invited to share the impressions and photos on the blog of this site

Our communality and good will is what can lead to change. Let's do a good job together!
